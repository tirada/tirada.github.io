Benefits of Becoming a Member

The benefits we offer our members are focused around SHPE National's development pillars: Community Outreach; Leadership; and Professional, Academic, and Chapter Development.

By providing meaningful activities in these areas, we assure students that they have made the right choice in joining a strong organization supported by strong companies.

Through our activities we enable our members to:

  • Acquire the leadership and team working skills required to success in a professional environment
  • Get involved on local events such as Community Outreach programs
  • Take part in National Conferences such as: the annual SHPE Conference (largest Hispanic socio-technical conference), the National Institute for Leadership Advancement (NILA), and the Eastern Technical Leadership Conference (ETLC)
  • Showcase their resumes to companies
  • Participate in professional development events and social awareness activities
  • Become eligible for different SHPE National and Company Scholarship opportunities
  • Promote themselves through direct exposure to companies and networking opportunities, local, and nationwide
  • Learn about research and graduate studies opportunities at the top STEM universities nationwide.

Advertising

The Advertising Committee coordinates with the External Secretary in order to relay information to the general membership, and must be accessible to all committee chairpersons for advertisement purposes. Members of this committee advertise all SHPE-Rensselaer activities and announcements through a variety of media.

Education

The education committee works with the Vice President to develop and maintain programs for the educational advancement of Chapter members. This committee's duties include to maintain the SHPE-Rensselaer Exam File and to assist the Executive Board to conduct seminars that are educational in nature.

Fundraising

The Fundraising Committee works closely with the Treasurer to coordinate programs in order to assist the Chapter's fund-raising efforts. Members on this committee will have a knowledge of the operating budget in order to ensure fund-raising efforts coincide with the needs of SHPE-Rensselaer.

Web

The Web Committee consists of a webmaster who works with the Internal Secretary in order to ensure the website is up to date with information relevant to chapter members.

Scholarships


Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers - Foundation Scholarship Program

SHPE offers scholarships and opportunities for Latino students pursuing degrees in engineering, math and science. Please note that you must submit a photo/video release form, a student profile form including a professional head-shot photo, updated resume, unofficial transcripts, and any other official documentation as required by the scholarship sponsor. All degree programs must be in the U.S. or Puerto Rico. Scholarship awards are disbursed in two payments during Fall and Spring terms. You must be a full time student for both terms to be eligible for both disbursements. More information on where and how to apply can eb found in the link below.

HENAAC Scholars Program

To qualify for a scholarship, applicants must be full time college STEM students (or high school seniors attending a college/university in the fall) active in the Hispanic community with an overall grade point average of at least a 3.0.

For additional information on how to participate in these programs, please visit this website: www.greatmindsinstem.org.

Voto Latino Innovators Challenge

The purpose of this challenge is to encourage more Latinos to pursue careers in technology. This tech competition will grant $500,000 in grants to the best 10-15 proposals.

We are pleased to announce that the 2nd annual SHPE RPI scholarship is now available! For those interested, applications are due on January 20th. This is your chance to prove that you are the most studious SHPE member and earn $300! Email your applications, questions, or concerns here.

Conferences


National Conference

SHPE National Conference takes place every fall. This year's conference took place in Kansas City, MO from November 1st-4th. A group of 26 active members from SHPE-RPI attended! Time to prepare for next year's conference!

Regional Leadership Development Conference (RLDC)

SHPE Regional Leadership Development Conference (RLDC) takes place every Spring. If you are interested in attending the conference, please prepare a letter of intent (LOI, see below for sample LOIs) to Marvin Rios explaining why going to conference will be a valuable experience. More information on the upcoming 2018 RLDC will be coming soon!

National Institute for Leadership Advancement (NILA)

NILA takes place every summer and is designed to give executive board members the necessary skills to foster leadership and chapter development. At the conference, these members have the opportunity to network with leaders from other chapters and to become certified chapter leaders. In the summer of 2017, SHPE-RPI sent 2017-2018 Vice President Marvin Rios to attend the National Institute for Leadership at Facebook Headquarters. For more information, please visit the NILA website.
